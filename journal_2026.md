If I train a NN on my synthetic moves and let is label the price of Bitcoin for example, would it conclude that the entire thing was generated from a completely random distribution, or will the network detect individual episodes which have positive and negative drift.

If there is a strong uptrending move that has lasted for a few minutes for example, there will be a large difference between it being generated by a distribution with a strong drift relative to volatility and a distribution with no drift and high volatility, even though it might look the same on a chart.
